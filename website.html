How to make a fortune teller!

Step 1.

Get an A4 sheet of paper

Step 2.

Fold a corner

Step 3.

Fold the other corner too

Step 4.

Cut the top line

Step 5.

Now you have a square sheet of paper

Step 6.

Fold corner to the center

Step 7.

Fold all corners to the center

Step 8.

Turn over

Step 9.

Turn corners to the center again

Step 10.

Fold all corners to the center

Step 11.

Turn over

Step 12.

Done!
